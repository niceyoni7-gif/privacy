Privacy Policy

Gmail Multi-Account Notifier by NiceYoni ("the Extension") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is accessed and how it is used.

Effective date: June 2026

This extension is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Google LLC. Gmail is a trademark of Google LLC.

Data We Access

  • Gmail data — unread inbox messages (sender, subject, snippet) via the Google Gmail API, only after you connect an account.
  • Google account email — to identify connected accounts.
  • Extension settings — stored locally (scan frequency, notification preferences, custom sounds, Do Not Disturb schedules).

How Data Is Used

  • Display emails in the extension popup.
  • Show desktop notifications for new mail.
  • Perform actions you request (mark read, archive, delete, star, spam).

Data Storage & Sharing

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. OAuth tokens are stored locally and refreshed through Google's secure OAuth flow. We do not operate external servers, do not sell your data, and do not transmit email content to third parties.

The Extension's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Third-Party Services

  • Google APIs — Gmail and OAuth (Google Privacy Policy).
  • Gravatar — optional sender profile images in the popup; only a hashed sender email is sent to gravatar.com.
  • Ko-fi — optional voluntary donation link; opens only when you click "Donate". No data is shared automatically.

Permissions

  • identity — Google sign-in via Chrome Identity API.
  • notifications — desktop alerts for new mail.
  • storage — local settings and email cache.
  • alarms — periodic background inbox checks.
  • offscreen — playback of custom notification sounds (Manifest V3).
  • tabs — close the privacy policy tab or open Gmail / donation pages when you choose.

Data Deletion

Remove an account in Extension Settings, or uninstall the extension to delete all locally stored data.

Contact

Questions about privacy: NiceYoni on Ko-fi or via the Chrome Web Store listing.
