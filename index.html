Privacy Policy for Gmail Multi-Account Notifier

Effective Date: June 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the "Gmail Multi-Account Notifier" Chrome extension developed under the trade name NiceYoni.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Our extension is designed to provide secure, local desktop notifications for new emails across multiple Gmail accounts. We care deeply about your privacy:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information, email contents, or credentials to any external servers.
  • Local Storage Only: All configuration settings, preferences, and custom sound data (including user-uploaded audio files) are stored strictly on your local device using Google Chrome's secure chrome.storage.local API.
  • Authentication: The extension utilizes official Google OAuth2 protocols directly through the Chrome Identity API to check for unread messages. Your tokens are managed securely by the browser environment and are never exposed to us or any third party.

2. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. Your data remains completely yours and never leaves your local browser environment.

3. Permissions Justification

  • identity: Used solely to securely authenticate with your Google accounts via official APIs.
  • notifications: Used to display visual alerts on your desktop when a new email arrives.
  • storage: Required to remember your sound preferences and custom notification settings locally.
  • offscreen: Used strictly to allow local audio playback of your chosen notification sounds within Manifest V3 architecture.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted directly on this public URL page.
